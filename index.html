Princess Arch Princess Arch is one of the smaller arches in the gorge. This doesn't mean however that is doesnt have a large personality or character. It would be a shame to miss out on the beauty and wonder of this little arch that has the benefit of easy access. Less than a mile from the nearest parking area. The easy trail and great view make this arch well worth the journey.
